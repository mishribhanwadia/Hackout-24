Soil Moisture Monitoring Systems

1. Capacitive Soil Moisture Sensor

The capacitive soil moisture sensor measures the soil's dielectric constant, which correlates with soil moisture content. It is highly accurate and less susceptible to corrosion compared to resistive sensors. Ideal for long-term monitoring and applications where high precision is required.

2. Resistive Soil Moisture Sensor

The resistive soil moisture sensor measures the electrical resistance between two probes buried in the soil. The resistance decreases as moisture content increases. This type of sensor is simple and cost-effective but may suffer from corrosion over time.
